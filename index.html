About IP Tech Pakistan

IP TECH is a trusted and recognized company and enjoys a wide array of client base using its expertise in top quality Solar Panels & Installation ,Fire Alarm, CCTV Cameras, Fire Detectors, Power Solution, Computer Networking,Biometric,RFID Access Control System and Sound System.

We enable our customers to meet their product requirements by acting as their procurement division, bringing in a flavor of a distribution company and maintaining the same service level of a personal support concern. This improves their revenue stream, without the need for a full-time procurement and support department, and still maintain the state-of-the-art resources that is critical for the day-to-day operations. Our Motto is "Let us put our experience and knowledge to work for you" leading to "Innovative & Optimum Solution Provider."
